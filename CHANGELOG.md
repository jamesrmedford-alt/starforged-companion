- Fixed: **Momentum could not drop below `momentumReset` — every −1 to a fresh PC was silently swallowed.** `actorBridge.applyMeterChanges` and `recalculateMomentumBounds` clamped momentum to `[momentumReset, momentumMax]`, but per the Starforged play kit (p. 1, "MOMENTUM: −6 to +10") and the vendor `MomentumField` (`MIN = -6`), `momentumReset` is the *target* value when burning momentum — not a lower bound during regular play. The bug surfaced on Forge as two Quench failures (`STARFORGED: Actor Bridge → applyMeterChanges → correctly decrements and restores momentum` and `STARFORGED: Move Pipeline → persistResolution → writes momentum delta`); both posted a `−1` change against a `value: 2 / resetValue: 2` actor and saw the value stick at 2.
